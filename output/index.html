Your insurance company wants to keep this from you

Insurance companies hate when you look at other options, but they can't stop you. The only thing they can do is not bring up how easily you can compare quotes (and raise your rate). That's what happened to Tommy.

Like most people, Tommy and his wife hadn't questioned their insurance rates. Frankly, the couple had steady jobs and were never stressed about money. But then the cost of living jumped, and Tommy started looking for ways they could save.

He first went to a well-known insurance company's site. After filling in the information for his Tesla and his wife's Mazda, he got a quote of $233.82/month for both cars.

But then he entered his zip code and car info at FinanceBuzz.com and was shocked. He got a quote from another big-name insurance company for $50.67/month — 78% lower than the other quote.
